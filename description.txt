Auto World Seeds Copy

by Nil256

----------------------------------------

The clipboard will be pasted the seed of world you'll create just after "Create" button on the world creation menu is pressed.

This mod is useful for reporting the seeds of the world with whose generation has some issue to a mod author.
This is also useful for world generation debug; you don't need to prepare random seeds by yourself for the debug anymore.
